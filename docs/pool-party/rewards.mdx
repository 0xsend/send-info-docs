# Pool Party Rewards

Pool Party rewards are live. The first distribution has been processed, with **1,500,000 $CC** allocated across approximately 800 participants who actively traded and provided liquidity on Canton through Send.

## How Claims Work

Pool Party rewards are distributed on a fixed cadence each month:

| Distribution Date | Cycle |
|-------------------|-------|
| 10th | Monthly cycle 1 |
| 20th | Monthly cycle 2 |
| 30th | Monthly cycle 3 |

Each cycle reflects real usage across trading and liquidity provision. If you participated, your rewards are available to claim in the app.

:::warning Claim Deadline
Claims expire at the end of the month at 00:00 UTC. Unclaimed rewards will not roll over — they are reallocated to future cycles. If you participated, claim before the window closes.
:::

## What Is Measured

Activity across the following areas is considered for reward eligibility:

- **Trading volume** across supported pairs
- **Liquidity provision** in Pool Party pools
- **Consistency of participation** over time

Activity is validated against on-chain data. Self-trading, wash volume, and any coordinated behavior designed to manufacture rewards are disqualified automatically. Organic usage only.

## Allocation

Pool Party was initially introduced as a 3M $CC rewards program. As activity scaled and new opportunities emerged across the ecosystem, a portion of that allocation is being extended into future campaigns.

| Phase | $CC Allocated | Status |
|-------|--------------|--------|
| Phase 1 | 1,500,000 $CC | Distributed |
| Future campaigns | Reserved | In progress |

This ensures rewards continue to align with usage, not just timing. Pool Party is not a one-time event — it is an active liquidity and trading layer that continues to reward participation as the network grows.

## Integrity

Pool Party rewards are calculated from verified activity. Inorganic volume — including self-trading, circular transactions, or any behavior designed to game the distribution — is excluded from eligibility.

## What Comes Next

Each cycle builds on the last: activity leads to liquidity, liquidity drives organic volume, and organic volume creates more opportunity.

- More campaigns are in motion
- More ways to participate are coming
- The next distribution is already in progress

The strongest signal remains the same: use the product and ensure you're [Send Verified](/docs/features/send-verified) for early access to new launches and rewards.
