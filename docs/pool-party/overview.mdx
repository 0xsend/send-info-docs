# Pool Party Exchange

<PoolParty />

## What is Pool Party?

Pool Party is Send's decentralized exchange (DEX) on Canton Network. It uses an Automated Market Maker (AMM) model — meaning trades execute against liquidity pools rather than a traditional order book. Anyone with a Canton Wallet can swap, bridge, and provide liquidity without intermediaries.

Pool Party launched in February 2026 and is the first AMM on Canton Network.

## Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Token Swaps** | Trade between CC, CUSD, and USDCx instantly on Canton Network |
| **USDC Bridge** | Bridge USDC from Ethereum to Canton as USDCx — no external tools needed |
| **Liquidity Provision** | Deposit token pairs into pools and earn a share of trading fees |

## Why Pool Party?

- **Be early to Canton DeFi** — Pool Party is the first AMM on Canton Network
- **Diversify beyond CC** — Swap between Canton Coin, CUSD, and USDCx
- **Direct USDC bridging** — Move USDC from Ethereum to Canton in one flow
- **Earn LP fees** — Provide liquidity and earn from every trade
- **Trade stablecoins natively** — Convert between CUSD and USDCx on-chain

## CUSD on Pool Party

CUSD is Send's privacy-first stablecoin on Canton Network. It's one of the most actively traded assets on Pool Party, available in two pairs:

- **CC / CUSD** — The primary trading pair for Canton Coin
- **USDCx / CUSD** — Stablecoin-to-stablecoin conversions

Learn more about CUSD in the [CUSD Stablecoin docs](/docs/cusd-stablecoin/overview).
