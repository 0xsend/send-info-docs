# Send Token FAQs

Answers to frequently asked questions about the $SEND token, utility, trading, and rewards.

## About $SEND

### What is the $SEND token?

$SEND is the native token of the Send ecosystem. It represents direct ownership in the project while unlocking exclusive opportunities and early product access.

### What utility does $SEND provide?

Earn $SEND tokens simply by using Send. These tokens represent direct ownership in the project while unlocking exclusive opportunities and early product access—making $SEND your gateway to the entire ecosystem.

### What blockchain is $SEND on?

$SEND is deployed on Base, an Ethereum Layer 2 network, providing fast and low-cost transactions.

## Acquiring $SEND

### How do I acquire $SEND?

Buy $SEND in send.app with USDC. Add money using Apple Pay or your debit card, then swap for $SEND. Coming soon: buy $SEND directly with CUSD in Privat.

### Where can I trade $SEND?

$SEND can be traded within the Send App and on supported decentralized exchanges on Base.

### Is there a minimum purchase amount?

There is no minimum purchase amount for $SEND. You can buy as little or as much as you want.

## Rewards & Benefits

### Are there rewards programs tied to $SEND?

Yes. When you hold $SEND, you unlock enhanced rewards across all Send products. Active users earn more, and your tokens multiply the benefits you receive from our ecosystem.

### How do rewards work with $SEND?

The more $SEND you hold and the more active you are in the ecosystem, the higher your reward multiplier. Rewards are distributed based on your participation level.

### Do I need to stake $SEND to earn rewards?

No staking is required. Simply holding $SEND in your Send wallet qualifies you for enhanced rewards.

## Tracking & Management

### How can I track my $SEND activity?

Track everything in Send App—your balance, transaction history, and rewards earned are all displayed in real-time.

### Can I transfer $SEND to other wallets?

Yes. $SEND is an ERC-20 token on Base and can be transferred to any compatible wallet. However, holding $SEND in your Send wallet maximizes your rewards benefits.

### Where can I see the $SEND price?

The current $SEND price is displayed in the Send App and on various crypto tracking platforms.

## Risks & Security

### What risks should I consider when holding $SEND?

$SEND is a crypto asset and can fluctuate in value. Keep long-term holdings in Send or Privat Safe using secure passkeys that are backed up to multiple devices. Never share your device credentials or passkeys with anyone.

### How do I secure my $SEND holdings?

- Use strong passkeys backed up to multiple devices
- Never share your passkeys or device credentials
- Be cautious of phishing attempts
- Only access Send through official channels (send.app)

### Is $SEND a security?

$SEND is a utility token designed to provide access to features within the Send ecosystem. Please consult your own legal and tax advisors regarding your specific situation.
