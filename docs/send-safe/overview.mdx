# Send Safe

Send Safe is a multisig on Canton Network. It lets teams and treasuries require multiple approvers on any transfer — with passkey-grade security and settlement that stays confidential by default. No seed phrases, no browser extensions.

## What You Get

- **Shared ownership** — require an `m-of-n` quorum before any asset can move
- **No seed phrases** — sign in with biometrics or a hardware security key the way you do with your banking app
- **Private transactions** — Canton Network keeps amounts and counterparties confidential
- **Institutional-grade security** — same WebAuthn standards banks and enterprises rely on

## What's Inside

Send Safe brings together several capabilities:

- **[Multisig](/docs/send-safe/safe)** — `m-of-n` approvals for shared treasuries
- **[Passkeys](/docs/send-safe/passkeys)** — biometric authentication that replaces seed phrases
- **[Bridging](/docs/send-safe/bridging)** — move assets between Base, Ethereum, and Canton
- **[Maintenance](/docs/send-safe/wallet-maintenance)** — manage devices, recovery, and rotation
- **[Send Connect](/docs/send-safe/send-connect)** — browser extension for connecting to Canton dApps

## Getting Started

1. Visit <a href="https://send.cantonwallet.com" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">send.cantonwallet.com</a> on a passkey-capable device.
2. Create or select the passkey you want to use (Face ID, Touch ID, Windows Hello, or a hardware security key).
3. Approve the enrollment prompt and confirm a recovery device if prompted.
4. Set up your multisig and start transacting on Canton Network.

## Security FAQ

- **Is face login secure?** Yes. Passkeys require possession of your trusted device and a successful biometric unlock. Spoofed photos or video replays fail because the device performs hardware-level liveness checks before releasing your credential.
- **Do attackers need my phone?** Yes. Without the physical device the passkey cannot be used, and even with the device they still need your biometric or device passcode.
- **What counts as a hardware key?** Any FIDO2/WebAuthn security key (like YubiKey or Feitian) that plugs into or pairs with your device. You can enroll one as an additional passkey for extra assurance.
