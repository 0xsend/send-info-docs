# Offramps

Send offramps let users move USDC out of the app and into a linked bank account. The feature is powered by Bridge for stablecoin-to-fiat conversion and Plaid for bank account linking.

## Availability

| Region | Conversion | Destination | Status |
|--------|-----------|-------------|--------|
| 🇺🇸 United States | USDC → USD (1:1) | Linked U.S. bank account | Live |
| 🌍 Other corridors | — | — | In development |

## Technology Partners

| Partner | Role |
|---------|------|
| **Plaid** | Bank account linking and verification |
| **Bridge** | Stablecoin-to-fiat conversion and settlement |

Both partners are regulated and widely used across U.S. fintech infrastructure.

:::info Disclaimer
Offramp services are provided by third-party partners. Availability, fees, limits, and processing times are subject to change. Send Foundation is not a bank, money transmitter, or financial institution and does not custody fiat currency. Transfers are subject to applicable laws and regulations.
:::
