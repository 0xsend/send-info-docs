# Offramps

Send's offramps let you move USDC from the Send App directly into your bank account. It's powered by Bridge for stablecoin-to-fiat conversion and Plaid for secure bank account linking.

## How It Works

1. Open the Send App and select **Offramp**
2. Connect your bank account via Plaid (one-time setup)
3. Enter the amount of USDC you want to convert
4. Review the conversion rate and fees
5. Confirm — funds settle to your bank account

USDC is converted to USD by Bridge and transferred to the linked account. Settlement times depend on your bank and the transfer method available.

## Availability

- **United States** — direct ACH transfers to a linked U.S. bank account
- **Other corridors** — additional regions are in active development

If you're outside the U.S., follow our updates for new corridor availability.

## Technology Partners

| Partner | Role |
|---------|------|
| **Bridge** | Stablecoin-to-fiat conversion and settlement |
| **Plaid** | Secure bank account linking and verification |

Both partners are regulated and widely used across U.S. fintech infrastructure.

## What You'll Need

- A verified Send account
- A U.S. bank account with online banking enabled
- USDC in your Send App balance

Bank linking is handled through Plaid's secure interface — Send does not see or store your bank credentials.

## Fees

Offramp fees and conversion rates are displayed before you confirm any transaction. Fees vary based on transfer speed and market conditions.

## Limits

Transfer limits depend on your account verification level and the linked bank. Larger transfers may require additional verification.

## Support

If you run into issues with an offramp transaction, reach out through the Send app or visit our community channels. For bank-specific issues, your bank may also have visibility into incoming transfers.

:::info Disclaimer
Offramp services are provided by third-party partners (Bridge and Plaid). Availability, fees, limits, and processing times are subject to change. Send Foundation is not a bank, money transmitter, or financial institution and does not custody fiat currency. Transfers are subject to applicable laws and regulations.
:::
