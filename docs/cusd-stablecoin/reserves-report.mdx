# CUSD Reserves Report

This page centralizes CUSD’s monthly reserve attestations, confirming circulating CUSD on Canton is fully backed 1:1 with frxUSD reserves managed through Brale.

## Attestation cadence and scope

- **Frequency**: Monthly statements validating circulating CUSD against held frxUSD reserves.
- **Coverage**: On-chain CUSD supply on Canton versus off-chain frxUSD custody balances.
- **Reserve backing**: frxUSD held 1:1 in institutional-grade custody; Brale issues and administers CUSD with ongoing monitoring.
