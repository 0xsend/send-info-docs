# CUSD Stablecoin Overview

**CUSD ($CUSD) - Privacy-First Stablecoin**

*CUSD redefines what a stablecoin can be: private, programmable, and safe for real-world use.*

## Executive Summary

Send offers CUSD ($CUSD), a USD-pegged stablecoin, on Canton Network via Brale for privacy-first transactions across institutional and retail use cases. CUSD enables confidential transactions by default while maintaining full regulatory compliance and 1:1 USD backing.

Reserve economics (currently ~4% annually, subject to change) are directed to treasury operations including $CC token repurchases after Brale's management fee. $CUSD adoption supports engagement across Canton's ecosystem of products.

### Key Value Propositions

- Privacy-first, safety-forward transactions with selective compliance disclosure
- Treasury operations include $CC token repurchases from reserve economics
- Canton Network ecosystem integration
- Enterprise-grade self-custody and regulatory framework

## Partnership Structure

**Send Distribution**: As the product owner of CUSD, Send will make CUSD the primary stablecoin to use on its app and own its distribution throughout Canton.

**Brale Issuance & Compliance**: Brale provides regulated native issuance and monitoring. CUSD is backed 1:1 with USD-denominated reserves.

**Reserve Economics**: Baseline reserve economics from short-duration U.S. Treasuries are directed to treasury operations including $CC token repurchases.

**Canton Alignment**: Capital-efficient reserves and ecosystem integration position CUSD as a Canton-aligned stablecoin across the network's apps and institutions.

## Integrations and Features

CUSD provides privacy-first infrastructure across Canton Network applications, pairing programmable mint/redemption via API with built-in safety controls for immediate on-chain confidential transactions.

### Core Privacy Features

- **Confidential Transfers**: Transaction amounts and counterparties remain private
- **Selective Disclosure**: Compliance reporting without compromising user privacy
- **API Access**: Programmatic integrations maintaining privacy guarantees

### Ecosystem Integrations

- **Send**: Private asset management and confidential DeFi interactions
- **Safe**: Multi-signature treasury management for enterprises
- **Pool Party**: Confidential automated market maker enabling low-slippage swaps between $CUSD and other Canton-native assets

## Rewards & Economics

All reserve economics attributable to CUSD are directed to the CUSD Treasury for $CC token repurchases and ecosystem operations. Brale takes a 10% management fee.

**Baseline pass-through**: Short-duration U.S. Treasury reserve economics are directed to treasury operations including $CC token repurchases.

**Canton alignment**: The Send×Brale structure supports the Canton ecosystem, positioning CUSD as the default stable asset on the network.

## Risk Management

### Regulatory

### Technical

**Outcome**: CUSD is designed to be a Canton-aligned stablecoin with compliance-focused infrastructure—powered by Send + Frax + Brale and integrated across Canton Network.
