# Ecosystem Fund

The CUSD Ecosystem Fund is a dedicated program that converts revenue generated from CUSD reserve economics into $CC — the native asset of Canton Network — and deploys it to grow the Canton ecosystem, attract partners, and expand CUSD adoption.

## Why CUSD?

CUSD is a USD-backed stablecoin on Canton Network that supports confidential transfers and selective disclosure. It is issued by Brale, a regulated issuer that handles KYC/AML compliance, and is available for programmatic mint/redeem via API.

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Confidential transfers | Transaction amounts and counterparties are private by default |
| Selective disclosure | Share specific information with regulators or auditors when required |
| Regulated issuance | Brale handles compliance, monitoring, and mint/redeem |
| Canton-native | Built for Canton Network with liquidity on Pool Party |
| API access | Programmatic integrations via Brale's issuance APIs |

## How the Fund Works

1. **Revenue Collection** — CUSD reserve yield (via Brale) and stablecoin revenue are collected monthly
2. **$CC Acquisition** — Funds are used to acquire $CC through Pool Party on the 7th, 14th, 21st, and 28th of each month
3. **Ecosystem Deployment** — Acquired $CC is allocated across growth initiatives that expand CUSD circulation and Canton adoption

All transactions are executed on-chain and verifiable.

## Allocation Framework

| Category | Target | Purpose |
|----------|--------|---------|
| **CUSD Adoption & Partnerships** | 40% | Integration grants and incentives for projects that natively adopt CUSD for payments, treasury, lending, or commerce |
| **Pool Party Liquidity** | 25% | Deepen CUSD trading pairs on Pool Party to reduce slippage and attract larger volume |
| **Builder Grants** | 20% | Fund developers and teams building applications on Canton using Send infrastructure |
| **Strategic Reserve** | 15% | Held for partnerships, co-investment opportunities, and future programs |

Allocations are targets and may be adjusted based on ecosystem needs. Actual allocations will be reported alongside each cycle.

## Partner Opportunities

The Ecosystem Fund is designed to support projects that grow CUSD circulation on Canton Network. If you're building on Canton, there are several ways to participate:

### Integration Grants

Projects that natively integrate CUSD — as a payment method, treasury asset, lending collateral, or settlement layer — can apply for $CC grants from the fund. The deeper the CUSD integration, the more value it creates for the ecosystem.

### Liquidity Co-Investment

The fund can co-invest alongside partners to bootstrap CUSD liquidity for new use cases on Pool Party, reducing the cold-start problem for new trading pairs or applications.

### Volume-Based Incentives

Partners who drive meaningful CUSD transaction volume may qualify for ongoing $CC allocations proportional to their contribution to CUSD circulation.

### Early Access

Active ecosystem partners get priority access to new Send infrastructure, Pool Party pairs, APIs, and Canton tooling as it ships.

Interested in partnering? Reach out through the Send app or community channels.
